ABOUT

HOW TO JOIN ?

There is no ‘formal’ way to join a club. You can go and participate in activities and events of any club. You will receive information about these via webmail. These activities are organized by the managers and conveners of respective clubs. In order to become a part of the council, you can take part in the recruitments that happen every year.
